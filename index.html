About CCSHC

We are a small, friendly, mixed club with players of all abilities and ages, ranging from 15-67 years. We welcome anyone with an interest in hockey to join us - perhaps you have been inspired by the performance of teams at the Olympics or Commonwealth Games, or you simply want to take up a team sport for the social and fitness benefits?

Cheltenham Civil Service Hockey Club (CCSHC) play in a mid-week 6 a side fun league which is ideal for beginners and returners. In addition, we have regular 11 a side weekend and mid-week games often with pre-training to help practice your skills and improve your fitness before the game.

What's in a name?
Don't be confused by the name...you don't need to be a civil servant to join us!
